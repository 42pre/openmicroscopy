OME's zoomable browser provides a compact overview of OME projects, datasets, and the images they contain. This manual will provide a quick introduction to using the browser to navigate through OME data.

The zoomable browser is designed to help users navigate the two main data containers in OME: projects and datasets. A project is generally seen as an ongoing effort involving multiple sets of images, known as. A dataset can also be included in multiple projects. In database terms, there is a many-to-many relationship between projects and datasets.A similar many-to-many relationship exists between datasets and images: any image may be found in multiple datasets.

OME also supports the notice of ownership of projects, datasets, images, and related entities. The zoomable browser will only show dataset objects that are owned by the user who is currently logged in to the system.

Projects and datasets are displayed in two areas in the zoomable browser window. The top section of the window contains projects listed by name, while the bottom section contains graphical representations of the images in various datasets. Each dataset is shown as a rectangular region, containing thumbnails for each of the contained images.
